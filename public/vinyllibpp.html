Privacy Policy for VinylLib

Effective Date: 03.03.2026

VinylLib (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data is used, how it is handled, and your rights when using the App.


1. Overview

VinylLib is a record collection tracking app. The App allows users to search and manage music releases using data provided by third-party services.

The App itself does not require an account and does not collect personal data unless explicitly stated.


2. Use of Third-Party Data (Discogs)

The App uses the Discogs API to provide music release information.

This may include:

  • Release titles and tracklists
  • Artist and label information
  • Formats and release details
  • Cover images

This data is provided by Discogs and is subject to their own terms and policies.

Important:

  • VinylLib is not affiliated with or endorsed by Discogs
  • Data is used in accordance with Discogs API Terms of Use
  • Some content may be contributed by Discogs users


3. Data Collection

3.1 Personal Data

VinylLib does not collect personal data, such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Location
  • Contacts


3.2 Local Data (Stored on Your Device)

The App may store the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your record collection
  • Notes you add
  • Record condition and pricing data

This data is:

  • Not transmitted to any server
  • Not shared with third parties
  • Fully controlled by you


4. Discogs API Requests

When using search or browsing features:

  • The App sends requests to Discogs servers
  • Your IP address may be visible to Discogs as part of standard web requests

We do not control how Discogs processes this data. Please refer to their privacy policy for details.


5. Data Sharing

VinylLib does not sell, share, or distribute your data to third parties.

The only external data exchange occurs when communicating with Discogs to fetch music data.


6. Data Retention

All user data stored by the App remains on your device until you delete the App or remove the data manually.


7. Security

We take reasonable measures to ensure that your data remains secure by keeping it stored locally and minimizing external data usage.


8. Children’s Privacy

VinylLib does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13.


9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with a new effective date.
